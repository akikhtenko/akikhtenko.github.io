Realized PnL crosses highlight periods when market participants realize more Bitcoin losses than profits for an extended time.

These crosses indicate significant fear and pain in the market when the price is heading down, all "smart" players have already taken most of the profits they wanted to take and the late buyers are selling in big losses.

Usually this represents a decent mid-term trade/investment opportunity on condition that Bitcoin is in a macro bull market.
